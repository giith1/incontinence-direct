Who can benefit from treatment
with EMS (Electromagnetic Seat)?

Electromagnetic Seat is ideal for both men and women suffering from incontinence
from weakened pelvic floor muscles. The therapy is especially helpful for:

After Childbirth: For many women, childbirth can and does diminish the strength
of the pelvic floor, so incontinence is a common problem to have post-birthing.
EMS makes it possible for new moms to recover and regain control safely and non
invasively - no surgery or drugs necessary.

Men With Prostate Problems: Prostate surgery and old age can send men down the
path toward urinary incontinence. The Electromagnetic Seat provides a safe,
non-invasive solution for building pelvic muscles, minimising leakage and
regaining control.

Older adults: As we get older, our muscles, including those in the pelvic floor,
tend to weaken. The Electromagnetic Seat provides muscle control enhancement
to older people, and relief of stress and urge incontinence.

Anyone with Incontinence: If you suffer from stress (leaking when coughing,
laughing or exercising) or urgency (strong needing to urinate), the Electromagnetic
Seat has been clinically proven to be an effective treatment for you to regain control!
